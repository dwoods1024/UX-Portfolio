About Me

Hello, I am Dylan.

Born and raised in Orinda California. I specialize in UX and UI Design. Driven by curiosity of solving user problems with robust solutions such as designing new products or redesigning existing ones with helpful features. My skills include user research, prototyping and interface design.



Skills



USER RESEARCH

Studying target users to discover their needs and pain points.



USABILITY TESTING

Testing prototypes with users to discover what aspects should be improved.



QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

Collect data through direct observation and study of participants.



PROTOTYPING

Designing mockups of products to test and validate design assumptions



STORYBOARDING

Drawing storyboards to tell the story of how the target user will use my product.



INTERACTION DESIGN

Designing products while focusing on facilitating the interactions between the device and the user.
